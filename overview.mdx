Engram is the agent-native trading terminal and intelligence layer built for Hyperliquid, a high-performance, decentralized Layer 1 blockchain optimized for trading. It pairs each trader with a purpose-built agent pre-loaded with an ever-evolving skill library, designed to compound as the collective intelligence engine accelerates. Out of the box, your agent comes armed with hundreds of real-time, quant-grade data streams from dozens of premium providers to analyze, synthesize explain, educate, and execute across every market class on Hyperliquid.

## The Core Loop

1. **Scan & Synthesize** – Your agent has instant access to comprehensive, real-time datasets for each of the 200+ markets on Hyperliquid, giving it genuine statistical edge in filtering noise and spotting early opportunities. You can ask directly – about any asset, thesis, or market condition – and the agent draws from the full depth of its data to answer.
2. **Translate & Propose** – Whether the agent found the setup or you brought the question, the output is the same: a plain-language breakdown of the opportunity, the supporting data, the risk parameters, confidence score, and the optimal entry and target levels.
3. **Decide & Execute** – You retain absolute control. Review the proposal, ask for a deeper walkthrough, then approve, modify, or reject it. Once confirmed, the agent executes directly through HyperCore — Hyperliquid's native on-chain execution layer.
4. **Monitor & React** – While a position is open, the same data layers that built the thesis continue running against it. If funding regimes shift, cohort positioning reverses, or liquidation clusters approach, the agent surfaces what matters and recommends adjustments – before the P&L tells you something changed.

The data infrastructure is the product. The agent is the interface. You make the decisions.
