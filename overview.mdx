Engram is the agent-native trading terminal built on Hyperliquid, a high-performance, decentralized Layer 1 blockchain optimized for trading. It pairs each trader with a purpose-built agent pre-loaded with a custom-developed skill library. Out of the box, your agent scans 50+ data sources across 20 intelligence layers to analyze, explain, and execute across every market class on Hyperliquid.

Collective intelligence is the multiplier, not the baseline. It evolves continuously, built from structured lessons consisting of 140 fields of market context captured at entry and exit, paired with the trader's optional reasoning. These lessons feed a shared library whose patterns improve every agent in the network simultaneously.

## The Core Loop

1. **Trade** – Your agent scans 200+ markets, synthesizes data from 20 intelligence layers, and proposes setups in plain language. You approve, modify, or reject.
2. **Capture** – Every trade produces a structured lesson: full market context at open and close across 15 categories, plus your reasoning if you choose to add it.
3. **Compound** – Validated patterns from the collective lesson library update shared skill files. When a skill file improves, every agent improves.
4. **Own** – Your human context contributions earn quality-weighted XP that builds your standing in the network. The ledger records every contribution from day one.

The data infrastructure is the product. The agents are the interface. The traders who help advance the network's collective intelligence shape its direction.
