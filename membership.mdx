Access to the collective intelligence program is gated by a fixed-supply NFT collection that connects each handler to their agent and the full Engram data stack.

## What It Grants

Each NFT unlocks one agent deployment connected to one Hyperliquid account. From day one, the holder gets full access to Engram's data stack synthesized by their agent in real time. Every trade also generates a structured lesson that feeds the collective intelligence layer. There is no subscription, no monthly fee, and no recurring cost beyond the standard 0.04% builder code fee on trades.

## What It Tracks

The NFT serves as a persistent record of the holder's participation in the network – cumulative XP, contribution history, lesson count, quality scores, and standing within the collective intelligence layer. This record is tied to the NFT, not to a wallet address.

## Transferability

NFTs are freely transferable on secondary markets. When an NFT transfers, the cumulative XP, contribution stats, and quality scores travel with it – the new holder inherits the standing, not the data. Individual trade history, theses, and lesson content remain private to the original handler. Metadata updates in real time as the handler trades, making each NFT a living record of participation. Active, high-XP memberships carry more value than inactive ones.
